HYMN LXVI

A hymn to Agni as the Sun

1The Asuras with iron nets, magicians, who roam about with
   hooks and bonds of iron,
  With wrath I make thy thralls, O Jātavedas. Come as a bolt foe-
   quelling, thousand pointed.
